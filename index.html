Welcome

Welcome to the official site of Jerry Knoelke — educator, mathematician, and author of Math Is Not Magic: Solve Your Math Word Problems. Jerry believes that every student can learn logic and understand math deeply when given a consistent visual routine.

Math Is Not Magic

Math Is Not Magic: Solve Your Math Word Problems is written to show that logic, not magic, drives learning. When neuroscience found that one consistent routine needs to be provided to reach the adolescent mind — and no one was doing this — Jerry stepped in to provide it.

The book helps students visualize math word problems as puzzles they can see and solve. Through a clear and repeatable method, Jerry demonstrates that math isn’t about tricks — it’s about understanding how the brain naturally learns.

One Universal Routine

An Analogy

Just imagine doing a child’s five piece jigsaw puzzle without seeing the pieces. If all you had to work with were the words describing the pieces it would be a complex challenge. But, if you see the pieces, it is just a simple child’s puzzle. It is the same with Math word problems. PRESS shows you how to make your Math word problem simplify to a child’s puzzle you can see for your mind to solve.
